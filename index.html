Last Will and Testament

To whom it may concern,
I, Lord Harrington, being of sound mind and questionable spirit, do hereby bequeath unto you a most curious legacy. Should you choose to uncover its secrets, your journey shall begin upon the reading of this document. Click below to begin you challenge. Upon completion if you shall put your neurons to the test, you will find a reward most fitting for your efforts.
Your first riddle lies within.

--Navigation Controls--
Use WSAD keys or arrow keys to move around the room.
Click with the mouse to interact with objects.
Press PageUp or PageDown to look up and look down respectively.
Use Space Bar to stop movement.
Optionally, you can click the arrows and navigation controls on the bottom right of the screen to navigate.
